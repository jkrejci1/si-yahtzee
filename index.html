Simple Yahtzee Game

In this game of Yahtzee, you will roll 5 different dice
and will be able to roll up to 3 times. Your score will
then be totaled along with possible special rolls like a
5 of a kind for example. In this single player game of
Yahtzee, the way to win is to score more than 50. Good luck!
Dice has already been rolled for you so you know where to look
when you roll them. This is a one time thing though!
